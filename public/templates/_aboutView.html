My Story
Hi, I’m Ale Davila. I am a web developer currently based in Austin, TX. I am a recent graduate from the General Assembly Web Development Immersive program and I hold a bachelors degree in Political Science from UT Austin.

While I was applying to law school, I became extremely inspired by web development I saw in the infamous show Silicon Valley. With my interest captured, I decided to research development and find out exactly what the title “developer” meant. This was the beginning of my journey of becoming a developer.

To my surprise, I fell in love with coding immediately. To me, programming is an art form and a way I like to express myself. With all of this in mind, I decided to take a leap of faith and go to a developer bootcamp instead of law school. I haven’t looked back since.

Currently, I am at IBM Design and am enjoying using my skills as a front-end developer. In the future, though, I would love to be involved on projects that are full-stack.
