My Story
Hello, my name is Ale Davila. I am a web developer currently based in Austin, TX. I am a recent graduate from the General Assembly Web Development Immersive program; I also hold a bachelors degree in Political Science. I started my journey in becoming a web dev after watching the infamous show Silicon Valley. At the time I was applying to law school and decided to research more into what it meant to be a developer. To my surprise I actually loved coding because I saw it as an art and a way to express myself. I decided to take a leap of faith and go to a bootcamp insted of law school. This was November of 2015, and I haven't looked back since. Now at IBM Design I love the focus of front-end, but I hope my next journey also involves the full-stack.
